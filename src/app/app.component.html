Introduction to "One Piece"

"One Piece" is based in a world where pirates roam the seas in search of a mythical treasure called "One Piece". The story is based on Monkey D. Luffy, a young and aspiring pirate who has the ability to stretch his body like rubber after eating a Devil Fruit. Luffy embarks on a journey to become the Pirate King by finding the One Piece and assembling a crew known as the Straw Hat Pirates.
